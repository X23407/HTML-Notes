SAMOSA RECIPE

A delicious, crispy and spicy snacks!

Ingredients

  • Dough Ingredients
    • Floor : 1/2 cups
    • Carrom seeds : 1/2 Tablespoon
    • Oil : 2-3 Tablespoon
    • Water : as needed
  • Filling Ingredients
    • Potatoes: 3-4 medium sized
    • Green peas : 1/2 cups
    • Ginger : 10-20gm finely chopped
    • salts : as per need
    • Spices : Garam masala, chilli powder, cumin seeds
  • Frying
    • Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
    1. Mix all-purpose flour, salt, carom seeds, and oil/ghee.
    2. Add water gradually and knead into a stiff dough. Rest 30 min.
  2. Make the filling
    1. Heat oil and other spices
    2. Add boiled potatoes. Mix well and let it cool
  3. Fill,seal and fry
    1. Roll the dough into circle and then make it in the cone shape.
    2. Stuff the cone with potato filling
    3. seal the top edge, making a triangularshape
    4. Fry the samosa in oil on medium flame untill golden brown and crispy
